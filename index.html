Ella Thompson

Ella Thompson recently graduated UNC Chapel Hill with double majors in Communication Studies (Media Production) and Asian Studies with a minor in Writing for the Screen and Stage. She's currently a videography and editing intern at NC Courage, a virtual editor for dance crew UNiCorn, and a part-time student in the UNC Fullstack Coding Bootcamp.

Following COVID-19, Ella hopes to move out to LA and persue a creative career including coding. She's active in sport and dance videography and would also like to film or edit short films. Additionally, she has newfound interest in front-end development and working with APIs - and hopes to learn more about game development.

Ella has experience in Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Ableton, and Blender. She hopes to apply her JavaScript skills to her special effects projects. She also has also been a boom operator and sound mixer on more than 20 student films. In addition to speaking French and studying Korean, Ella is learning animation.
