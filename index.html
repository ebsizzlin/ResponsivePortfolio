About Ella

Ella Thompson recently graduated UNC Chapel Hill with double majors in Communication Studies (Media Production) and Asian Studies with a minor in Writing for the Screen and Stage. She's currently a videography and editing intern at NC Courage and a development paid-intern at Pressman Film.

Following COVID-19, Ella hopes to move out to LA and persue a creative career including coding. She's active in sport and dance videography and would also like to film or edit short films. Additionally, she has newfound interest in website and app development after spending a semester creating a website for a class.

Ella has experience in Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Ableton. She also has also been a boom operator and sound mixer on more than 20 student films. In addition to speaking French and studying Korean, Ella is dyscalculic.
